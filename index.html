What is a Datathon?

A Datathon is a competitive event where participants apply data science, machine learning, and statistical techniques to solve real-world problems using large datasets. Teams work within a limited time, to analyze data, create models and presentÂ solutions.

What is Datanyx?

Datanyx'24 is a 24-hour datathon organized by IEEE SMC MJCET and AWS Cloud Club MJCET, where teams use their expertise in machine learning, data analysis and creativity to submit innovative solutions.

Food will be provided. Participants will stay in a hall with faculty supervision. However, transportation is not provided, and participants must arrange it themselves.

Participants must be undergraduate students, forming teams of 2 to 4 members.

Welcome to the AWS Cloud Club at MJCET! Our club is focused on empowering students with the knowledge and practical skills to navigate the world of cloud computing, particularly through the use of Amazon Web Services (AWS). By fostering a collaborative and learning-focused environment, we provide members with the tools to master cloud infrastructure, AI integration, and cloud deployment. Since its formation, the AWS Cloud Club has drawn students from diverse departments, promoting teamwork and cross-disciplinary innovation. Our mission is to equip students with the expertise needed to leverage cloud technologies effectively, preparing them for the future of tech innovation.
